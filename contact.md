## Join Our Network

We are always looking for collaboration opportunities with:

- **Researchers:** Join our research activities and contribute to publications
- **Students:** Participate in training schools and Short-Term Scientific Missions (STSMs)
- **Industry Partners:** Apply our methodologies to real-world problems
- **Funding Agencies:** Support our research and networking activities

If you are interested in joining as a member or participant, you can sign up for an [e-COST account](https://e-services.cost.eu/) and join one of the [working groups](https://www.cost.eu/actions/CA24148/#tabs+Name:Working%20Groups%20and%20Membership). You can also directly contact the [working group](/working-groups) coordinator.

## Get in Touch

We welcome inquiries from researchers and students interested in EEG. For up-to-date administrative information, you can also check the description of the project on the [COST Action website](https://www.cost.eu/actions/CA24148).
